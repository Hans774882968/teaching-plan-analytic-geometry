title: $f(x) = x^4+ax^3+bx^2+cx+d$ 且 $f(1)=10$ , $f(2)=20$ , $f(3)=30$
---

求`f(10) + f(-6)`。

构造

$$
g(x) = f(x) - 10x
$$

则

$$
g(1) = g(2) = g(3) = 0
$$

设第四个根为k，则

$$
g(x) = (x-1)(x-2)(x-3)(x-k)
$$

于是

$$
g(10) + g(-6) = 9*8*7*(10-k) + (-7)*(-8)*(-9)*(-6-k)
$$

$$
 = 9*8*7*(10-k+6+k) = 9*8*7*16 = 8064
$$

所求为`f(10) + f(-6) = 8064 + 100 - 60 = 8104`
